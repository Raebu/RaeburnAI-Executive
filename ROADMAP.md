- Stable open-source foundation.
- Mock connector architecture.
- API, dashboard, CI, Docker and security baseline.

- Real Gmail and Microsoft 365 inbox connectors.
- Google Calendar and Microsoft Calendar connectors.
- HubSpot and Salesforce CRM connectors.

- Background briefing scheduler.

- Enterprise SSO and RBAC.
- Multi-tenant deployment model.
- Executive mobile digest.
- Slack, Teams and email briefing delivery.
- Human-in-the-loop action approval workflows.
- Advanced competitor intelligence and market monitoring.
